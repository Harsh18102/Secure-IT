How to Make Your Password Strong

Creating a strong password is crucial for protecting your accounts and sensitive information. Here are some tips to make your password strong:

  • Include a mix of case letters.
  • Add numbers and special characters.
  • Avoid using common words or phrases.
  • Length should be at least 12 characters long.
  • Use a unique password for each accounts.
