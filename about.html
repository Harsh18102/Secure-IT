About Secure IT Encryption/Decryption

Secure IT Encryption/Decryption is a project aimed at providing robust security measures for protecting sensitive data. It offers encryption and decryption functionalities for various types of files, including text, audio, and images. The project uses advanced encryption algorithms to ensure data confidentiality and integrity.
